Home Info

Home Info is an MLS entry app for Android that I developed for my parents' realty company, RealtyCool, to ease the process of data entry. I began development with very little Android Development experience and used this opportunity to hone my skills. I completed the first version in just over two weeks, including iconography and PDF export.

Unfortunately, a successful release was not in the cards. I made the mistake of developing the app on a device without a backup or remote repository. A small spill later, and the app was gone. As devistating as this was at the time, it gave me an opportunity to make the app again with all that experience under my belt.

The next edition of the app was completed in just under two weeks (with many backups) and boasted much cleaner code and more features including printing capability as well as a refreshed user interface.
